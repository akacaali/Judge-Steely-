Meet Judge Steely

AI Powered Powerlifting Judge

Judge Steely brings cutting-edge AI technology to the world of powerlifting. With the power of artificial intelligence, Judge Steely can analyze your form and technique with precision, helping you perfect your lifts.

Imagine having a virtual judge that can assess your squat depth, bench press form, and deadlift lockout, all with just a simple video. Whether you’re a seasoned lifter looking to refine your technique or a beginner striving for proper form, Judge Steely is here to help.

Stay tuned for the upcoming iOS app, where you’ll be able to easily upload your training videos for instant feedback from Judge Steely. Take your training to the next level with the power of AI.
